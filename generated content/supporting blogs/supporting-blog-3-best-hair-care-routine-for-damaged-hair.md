# The Best Hair Care Routine for Damaged Hair (2026 Updated)

Damaged hair can be restored with the right routine. Here’s a simple, science-backed care system tailored for Indian hair.

---

## 🧴 Step 1: Use a Sulphate-Free Shampoo  
Avoid shampoos with SLS/SLES—they strip natural oils.

---

## 🧴 Step 2: Deep Conditioner  
Use once or twice a week to restore moisture.

---

## 🧪 Step 3: Hair Serum  
Reduces frizz, adds shine, and protects hair.

---

## 💆 Step 4: Weekly Hair Spa  
A hair spa helps restore moisture and rebuild damaged bonds.

**Habibs Recommendation:**  
Hydra Repair Spa — ideal for chemically treated or dry hair.

---

## 🫶 Bonus Tips  
- Avoid heat styling  
- Do oiling 1–2 times a week  
- Trim every 8–10 weeks
