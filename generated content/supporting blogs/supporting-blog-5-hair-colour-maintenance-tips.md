# Hair Colour Maintenance Tips — Make Your Colour Last 2x Longer

DYI routines and cheap shampoos can quickly fade your expensive hair colour.  
Here’s the best routine to make your colour last longer and stay vibrant.

---

## 🎨 1. Use a Colour-Safe Shampoo  
Normal shampoos strip the colour.

---

## 💧 2. Avoid Hot Water  
It opens the cuticle and washes out colour faster.

---

## 🧴 3. Use Leave-In Conditioner  
Keeps hair hydrated and shiny.

---

## ♻ 4. Reduce Heat Styling  
Heat fades colour faster—always use heat protectant.

---

## 🔁 5. Touch-Up Every 8–10 Weeks  
Maintains vibrancy and avoids patchiness.
