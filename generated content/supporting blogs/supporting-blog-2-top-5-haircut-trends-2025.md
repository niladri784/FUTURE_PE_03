# Top 5 Haircut Trends Dominating 2025

Whether you're refreshing your look or trying something bold, 2025 is filled with trendy, stylish haircut options for all face shapes.

---

## ✂ 1. The Airy Korean Bob  
Light, soft, and bouncy—perfect for a youthful, modern look.

**Best for:** Round, oval, and heart-shaped faces.

---

## ✂ 2. Curtain Bangs  
A timeless trend that frames your face and adds volume.

**Best for:** Almost every face shape.

---

## ✂ 3. Layered U-Cut  
Soft, flowy layers that give movement without removing length.

**Best for:** Long hair & wavy hair.

---

## ✂ 4. French Bob  
Short, chic, and textured.

**Best for:** Square and oval faces.

---

## ✂ 5. Shag Cut  
Messy, edgy, and full of personality.

**Best for:** Wavy/curly hair.

---

## 💡 Why These Trends Work  
They are easy to style, photograph beautifully, and suit Indian hair textures.
