## ✅ What Is Hair Smoothening?
Hair smoothening is a **chemical hair treatment** that relaxes curls and reduces frizz by modifying the hair’s hydrogen bond structure.  
It gives:

- Soft, silky texture  
- Shiny finish  
- Straight to semi-straight look  
- Easy combing & styling  

Unlike permanent straightening, smoothening keeps some natural movement so the hair doesn’t look flat or poker-straight.

---

## 🧪 Types of Smoothening Treatments
### **1. Chemical Smoothening**
Uses chemical relaxers.  
Best for: Very frizzy or curly hair.

### **2. Cream Smoothening**
Gentler on hair.  
Best for: Wavy or mildly frizzy hair.

### **3. Protein-Infused Smoothening**
Adds protein + smoothens hair.  
Best for: Slightly damaged or dry hair.

---

## 💡 Who Should Get Hair Smoothening?
You’re an ideal candidate if you have:

- Frizzy hair  
- Dry or rough hair  
- Wavy or curly hair you want to relax  
- Hair that is difficult to style daily  
- Busy routine with low-maintenance needs  

Avoid smoothening if:

- Your hair is **highly damaged**  
- You have **active scalp issues**  
- You recently colored/bleached your hair heavily  

---

## 🛑 Risks & Side Effects
Like any chemical process, there can be side effects:

- Dryness  
- Breakage if not maintained  
- Fading of hair color  
- Loss of natural volume  
- Temporary scalp sensitivity  

But these can be controlled with proper aftercare and professional application.

## 🧴 Aftercare Routine (Very Important)
To maintain smoothening results:

### **1. Use sulphate-free shampoo**
Chemicals in normal shampoo can undo the treatment.

### **2. Avoid tying hair for 3 days**
This prevents dents and bends in newly treated hair.

### **3. Oil hair only 7 days after**
Before that, it may interfere with the treatment.

### **4. Weekly hair mask or spa**
Restores moisture and keeps hair shiny.

### **5. Protect from heat**
Use a heat protectant whenever styling.
